What We Do

The Crypto Club was founded Fall of 2021 as the leading foundation for normalizing cryptocurrency education for all students on campus. Acting as the first official crypto-technical analysis club of its kind. TCC provides students with invaluable knowledge about cryptocurrency and all technology associated with it.

As the first official crypto-technical analysis club of its kind, our goal is to teach students to spot trends in financial charts, present investment ideas, and educate them on how to buy and sell different cryptocurrencies.
